Q: Why Can’t I Connect To Nearby Devices?

A: In order to connect to nearby devices, you need to ensure 3 things.
1. Download and install the latest version of iOS
2. Download and install the latest version of Dust
3. Enable Bluetooth and/or Wi-Fi â€” Dust works best when both Bluetooth and Wi-Fi are enabled but will work with either one.

Q: Can I Connect To Someone Over The Internet?

A: No, Dust only allows you to connect to other iOS devices running Dust that are within Bluetooth range or on the same Wi-Fi network.

Q: Does Does Save My Gestures?

A: No, Dust trails are only in the application for 6 seconds, then they are gone forever.

Q: How Many Devices Can Connect At The Same Time?

A: Apple’s Documentation says 8.
